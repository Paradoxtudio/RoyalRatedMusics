RoyalRatedMusic

RRM is an R&B group collaboration including singer-songwriter GlenRoyal and dynamic vocalist Rated Genius; together forming ‘Royal Rated Music’. Local Toronto artists and die hard music lovers, they came together in 2020 to harmonize their unique style creating fresh yet nostalgic R&B Neo-Soul music. RRM’s sound is a reflection of their all time favourite hit maker’s like Boyz II Men, Joe, Jagged Edge, and H-Town to name a few. The foundational components of R&B allow GlenRoyal to experiment with Pop and Trap-Soul vocals while mixing Gospel in his songwriting. Rated Genius the main singer brings Soul & Hiphop elements which anchors the duo sonically. RRM has been working to develop their signature sound for the past year with Savage Sound Studio Recordz and they have a solid repertoire of unreleased music for 2022. The group's energetic chemistry is most highlighted with rifts, falsettos, and harmonization.Their latest single “We Ain’t Vibin” released December 27th 2021 closed out the year and is streaming on all platforms along with the “We Ain’t Vibin” music video on YouTube. RRM is an independent unsigned group working with some of Toronto’s well established studios, engineers, and producers like Smickentbeats, Top Tracks, and many more. Look out for more new music and RRM’s upcoming singles. Follow RRM on Instagram and subscribe to their YouTube channel to stay up to date with their latest music!
